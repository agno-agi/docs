## Connect your AgentOS

1. Click the team/organization dropdown in the top navigation
2. Click **"+"** next to "Add new OS"

## Choose Environment

| Environment | Use case | URL format |
|-------------|----------|------------|
| **Local** | Development | `http://localhost:8000` |
| **Live** | Production | Your HTTPS URL |

## Configure Connection

- **Endpoint URL**: Where your AgentOS is running
- **OS Name**: A descriptive name (e.g., "Development OS", "Production Chat Bot")
- **Tags** (optional): Organize instances with labels like `development`, `production`, `chatbot`

## Connect

Click **"CONNECT"**. If successful, your OS appears in the dashboard.

## Verify Connection

Once connected:

- **Status**: Shows "Running" in the platform
- **Features**: Chat, Knowledge, Memory, Sessions are accessible
- **Agents**: Your configured agents appear in the chat interface
