**Knowledge** provides agents and teams with domain-specific context, enabling accurate and relevant responses beyond what the model learned during training.

Common use cases include company documentation (HR policies, product specs), database schemas for text-to-SQL, FAQ databases, and any domain-specific information your agents need.
